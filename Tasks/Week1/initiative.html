About Eureka!

Eureka is a business proposal pitching event organized by RJ College E-Cell, inspired by the Eureka competition by E-Cell IIT Bombay. It offers students the opportunity to pitch innovative business ideas to industry experts. The best ideas win prizes, recognition, and opportunities for further development.
