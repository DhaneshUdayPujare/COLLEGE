General Quiz Rules

  • Time Limits: Participants are required to complete the quiz within a specified time. Each question or the entire quiz may have a timer. Exceeding the time limit may result in disqualification or automatic submission.
  • Single Attempt Only: Most quizzes allow only one attempt per participant to ensure fairness. Retakes are typically not permitted unless explicitly mentioned.
  • No Outside Help: Participants are prohibited from using external resources like books, the internet, or assistance from others during the quiz unless stated otherwise (e.g., open-book quizzes).
  • Device Usage: Participants should only use authorized devices for the quiz. Switching between tabs or applications may result in disqualification if monitored.
  • Honesty and Integrity: Any attempt to cheat, manipulate results, or disrupt the quiz process will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Answer Submission: Answers must be submitted within the provided interface, and clicking "Submit" is final. Changes to responses after submission are typically not allowed.
